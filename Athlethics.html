What Makes Us Unique?

Opened in 1969, Mayfield is a vibrant, inclusive, creative, and high energy environment with very high expectations for students both academically, and socially. We expect our students to strive for academic success while also striving for success in their extra-curricular pursuits. As the Regional Arts School for students north of the 401 Mayfield offers a deep and immersive experience for students study the arts including, Drama, Music, Visual Art and Dance. Beginning in September 2024 we are excited to offer a Specialist High Skills Major (SHSM) Program in Business. This will provide any interested students with valuable business skills applicable in many occupations.
